  • Quick Guide

    1. You have to be a member of Love Live! School Idol Festival - Trading & Giveaway Group in order to use this application at all.
    2. If your identification is not logged in, click on Log in using Facebook to obtain your usage right to the app.
    3. Click on your name to see more information about yourself in the profile page.
    4. To claim a giveaway: Enter the Giveaway ID given to you by the person who post that giveaway.
    5. Only "Contributor" account can submit new giveaways and view their submitted giveaways. Contact an Admin to become one.
    6. It is strongly recommended that you post your giveaway ID to the group instantly after a submission. You shouldn't rely on this app as your sole method of storing transfer codes.
    7. When your giveaway is "claimed", please be so kind as to announce so on your Facebook's post, so that others won't have to try claiming it again.
    8. Contact me if any problem arises.
